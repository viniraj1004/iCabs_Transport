Ensuring Health & Hygiene

Wellness is the topmost priority for all of us. In this pandemic situation, to keep our economy growing
we must need to stay safe, maintain hygiene, and plan for a better future.
So for a healthy environment, we have updated our guidelines to fight the pandemic.

Keeping It Transparent

The customers or admins can also get all the updates of checks and other key details from their dashboard

Intelligent Routing To Avoid Hotspots

Now our services are open for the COVID free zones and as per the statistics, we will
also plan the COVID free routes only. The services from the contaminated zones
are not considered and will auto-removed to ensure safety.
The driver and the admin team will be alerted while the cab enters the contaminated
or red zone.
